The CollegeTips Digital Literacy Program is a heartfelt gift to empower your parents and elder family members with the skills to thrive in today’s digital world. Designed for seniors who may find technology daunting, this program makes learning simple, fun, and accessible.

  • Master Digital Tools: Learn to use apps like Zomato, Ola, and Google Maps with ease.
